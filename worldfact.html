A few facts about the world we live in.

Earth is the third planet from the sun and is the largest of the terrestrial plaets. Unlike the other planets in the solar system that are named after classic deities the Earth's name comes from the anglo-Saxon world erda which means ground or soil. The Earth was formed approximately 4.54 billian years ago and is the only known planet to support life.
